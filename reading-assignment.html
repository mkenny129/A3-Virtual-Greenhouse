Spider Plant

The spider plant, or Chlorophytum comosum, is an African plant of the Asparagaceae family commonly grown as an ornamental houseplant. The most popular varieties feature long grassy green/white striped leaves. Spider plants are easy to grow and thrive under a variety of conditions.

Aloe Plant

Aloe vera, or Aloe barbadensis miller, is a succulent plant used in alternative medicine. The name aloe vera was derived from the Arabic word “Alloeh”, meaning a “shining bitter substance” and vera came from the Latin word “vera”, meaning “true”. People have used it for thousands of years for healing and softening the skin.

Jade Plant

The jade plant, or Crassula ovata, is a known as a good luck charm in Asia thought to activate financial energies. A flowering jade plant reflects well on the owner and symbolizes great friendship, luck, and prosperity.
